What is NextWatch?

Discover what moves you

NextWatch is a personalized discovery platform built for anime and movie lovers. We understand that finding the right thing to watch can feel overwhelming — endless scrolling, vague recommendations, and zero context. We built something different.

Tell us what you love, and we'll learn your taste. Whether you're chasing emotional drama, high-octane action, or slow-burn romance — NextWatch surfaces the stories that will resonate with you specifically.

The Team

Built with Passion by two anime nerds

NextWatch was built as a college project by two people who are genuinely passionate about anime and cinema. Here's a little message from each of us.

🧑‍💻
Rahul Saggu
Co Developer · Content Curator

"I've spent more hours watching anime than I'd like to admit — and even more hours arguing about which ones are actually worth watching. NextWatch started from a simple frustration: every recommendation platform either flooded me with things I'd already seen, or things I'd never care about. I wanted something that actually knew my taste. So I built it. I hope when you use NextWatch, it feels less like a database and more like getting a recommendation from a friend who genuinely gets you."
